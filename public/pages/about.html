About Us

Built on the Gulf Coast.
Built for Main Street.

TurnkeyAI Services is a veteran-founded technology company based in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi. We build AI-powered websites that help local service businesses compete and grow.

The Problem We Solve

Most local businesses — plumbers, electricians, restaurants, salons, contractors — know they need a website. But the options aren't great. Pay a web agency $3,000–$10,000 and wait weeks. Use a DIY builder and spend hours fighting templates. Or just skip it and hope word-of-mouth holds up.

Meanwhile, customers search Google before they call anyone. A business without a strong online presence is invisible to the people who need it most.

TurnkeyAI fixes that. We built a platform that generates a professional, industry-specific website in under 24 hours — complete with AI chat, online booking, SEO, and a client dashboard — for $99 a month. No setup fee. No tech skills required. The business owner fills out a 10-minute form, reviews a preview, clicks approve, and they're live.

Who We Are

TurnkeyAI Services was founded by George Dickson, an Army veteran, former law firm CEO, and lifelong entrepreneur based in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi. George has spent decades building and running businesses, and he understands firsthand what small business owners need: results, not complexity.

The platform is built on modern infrastructure — Node.js, PostgreSQL, Cloudflare Pages, and AI services — but none of that matters to the client. What matters is that they get a great-looking website that actually brings in customers, and they don't have to think about the technology behind it.

🎖️ Veteran-Founded. Mississippi-Built.

TurnkeyAI Services is proudly headquartered in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi. We're part of the Innovate Mississippi ecosystem and committed to helping Gulf Coast businesses — and local service businesses everywhere — get the online presence they deserve.

Our Approach

We believe small businesses shouldn't need a marketing department or a six-figure budget to look professional online. Our platform handles the entire lifecycle: intake, site generation, deployment, AI chat, content updates, and ongoing management. One flat monthly fee. No surprises.

For businesses that want to grow further, we offer blog content, social media management, and our Mini-Me AI Avatar — a digital version of the business owner that greets website visitors 24/7.
