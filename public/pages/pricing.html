FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees, ever. You pay the monthly rate starting when your site goes live.
How long does it take to go live?
Most sites go from intake form to live website in under 24 hours. You'll receive a preview first and can request changes before approving.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Month-to-month on all plans.
What if I already have a domain?
No problem — we'll connect your existing domain to your new site. Just let us know during signup.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None whatsoever. You fill out a form, review your preview, click approve. That's it. We handle everything else.
What's the Mini-Me AI Avatar?
It's a personal AI-powered video avatar that represents you on your website 24/7. You record a short clip, and we turn it into a digital version of you that greets visitors and answers questions. $59/month as an add-on, or included in the Full Package.
