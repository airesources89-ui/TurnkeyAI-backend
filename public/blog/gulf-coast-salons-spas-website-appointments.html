Why Gulf Coast Salons and Spas Are Losing Appointments to Competitors With Better Websites

💇 Week 8 of 12 · 4 min read · Gulf Coast, MS

Your talent is undeniable. Your clients love you. But when someone new moves to Gulfport and Googles 'best hair salon near me,' do they find you — or do they find your competitor who has a beautiful website with online booking?

Online Booking Is No Longer Optional

A growing percentage of customers — especially anyone under 40 — prefer to book appointments online rather than calling. If you don't offer online booking, you're losing those customers before they ever meet you. A professional website with a simple booking form captures them while they're interested, at 10pm on a Tuesday when your phone isn't answered.

Your Portfolio Is Your Sales Tool

Salons live and die by visual proof. Before and after photos, color work showcases, special occasion styles — these sell appointments better than any description ever could. A website with a clean, mobile-optimized photo gallery turns browsers into booked clients.

Stand Out on the Gulf Coast

Biloxi, Gulfport, and Pass Christian have no shortage of salons and spas competing for the same customers. The businesses that consistently win new clients have one thing in common: they're easy to find online, easy to evaluate, and easy to book. Your website is how you check all three boxes.
