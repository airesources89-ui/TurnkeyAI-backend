How Missed-Call Text-Back Saves Mississippi Businesses
Thousands Per Year

📅 June 11, 2026 ⏱ 4 min read 📍 Mississippi

The average service contractor in Mississippi misses 3–8 calls per day. Each missed call is a potential customer who — if they don't hear back within minutes — calls the next business on their list. At an average job value of $200–500 for home services, missing even 2 jobs per week from missed calls costs a contractor $20,000–50,000 per year in lost revenue. Missed-call text-back is the automated solution that stops this bleeding immediately.

Nobody Leaves Voicemails Anymore

Voicemail response rates have dropped dramatically — most studies show that fewer than 20% of callers leave a voicemail when they reach one. The other 80% simply hang up and call the next business. If your missed call strategy is "they'll leave a voicemail and I'll call back," you're losing the 80% who didn't. Missed-call text-back catches those 80% by immediately sending a text — the communication channel they actually prefer.

"80% of callers who reach your voicemail hang up without leaving a message. Missed-call text-back catches them before they call your competitor."

Speed Is Everything in Missed-Call Response

Research consistently shows that lead response time is the most important factor in conversion — leads contacted within 5 minutes convert at dramatically higher rates than leads contacted within an hour. TurnkeyAI's missed-call text-back responds within seconds of a missed call, while the prospect is still actively thinking about their need and hasn't yet called anyone else. That speed advantage wins jobs.

What the Text Says Matters

A missed-call text response should immediately establish credibility and invite a response — not just say "sorry we missed your call." TurnkeyAI customizes your missed-call text response to include your business name, a brief description of what you do, and a simple question to continue the conversation: "Hi, this is Gulf Coast Plumbing — sorry we missed your call! Are you dealing with a plumbing emergency or looking to schedule a service? We can typically get to you same-day." That's a text people respond to.
